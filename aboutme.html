ABOUT ME

Who Is Brian Makau?

Brian Makau is a Junior Developer on a journey to be among the best developers of his time. Like many other people I started out life from a very humble beginning, but that's no reason to hold me back from achieving the absolute best that I could.

My Story

I started out life as a smart kid. Not to toot my horn or anything but from a young age my friends, parents and teachers told me I had aptitude and was able to achieve great things. Unfortunately life was not that easy growing up. I came up in a very hostile home. Life always threw obstacles at me but I was and still am a fighter, so everything tossed my way, I was able to combat. I tried real hard and lucky enough I'm where I am today. The loss of my only parent was one of the hardest things to ever happen to me because she was my whole support system. I do not say all this to seek pity, but rather to show everyone that even they can make it if they're determined.

My Education

My education is the most important part of my life. School was like an escape to me when I was young and I got adapted quite fast. I was a gifted student in primary school and even in high school I maintained the consistency. I graduated primary school in 2012 and perfomed fairly well. I enrolled to Mumbuni Boys' High School where I graduated from in 2016 and scored a B(plain) grade. After that I enrolled in Mount Kenya university to pursue a Bachelor Degree in Energy and Environmental Engineering. I finished this in 2020 and am now a proud student of Moringa School under the Access program.

Why Programming?

I decided to do programming out of curiosity and passion to know how to code. I really enjoyed the company of my developer friends and I always wanted to be involved in what I saw them do. I did not hesitate to apply to Moringa School when I got the opportunity and luckily I made it. Coding is one of the things that makes me extremely happy. I enjoy the thrill it gives me and the challenge it poses. The idea that I cannot learn all there is about coding is amazing. To get to meet with different people, to learn from them and to maybe teach them a thing or two is purely amazing. I couldn't ask for anything else!
